Chapter: Our Body

Our body is made of many parts. The head, hands, legs, eyes, ears, and nose are important body parts.

Eyes help us see. Ears help us hear sounds. The nose helps us smell and breathe.

We should keep our body healthy by eating good food, drinking clean water, and keeping ourselves clean.

Chapter: Plants

Plants are living things that grow in soil. They need sunlight, water, and air to live.

Plants have roots, stem, and leaves. Roots absorb water from the soil. The stem holds the plant upright.

Plants give us fruits, vegetables, and flowers.

Chapter: Animals

Animals live around us. Some animals are pets like dogs and cats. Some animals live in forests like lions and elephants.

Animals need food, water, and shelter to live.

We should protect animals and take care of them.

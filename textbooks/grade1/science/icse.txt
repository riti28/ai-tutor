Chapter: Our Body and Health

Our body helps us do many activities like walking, running, and playing.

The eyes help us see beautiful things. The ears help us hear sounds. The nose helps us smell flowers and food.

We should take care of our body by eating healthy food, exercising, and keeping ourselves clean.

Chapter: Plants and Trees

Plants and trees grow around us. They are living things.

Plants need sunlight, water, air, and soil to grow.

Different parts of a plant are roots, stem, leaves, flowers, and fruits.

Plants give us oxygen, food, and shade.

Chapter: Animals and Birds

Animals and birds are living things. They move, grow, and need food and water.

Some animals live on land like dogs and cows. Birds fly in the sky.

Animals and birds are part of nature and we must take care of them.

Chapter: My Body

Our body has many parts. The main parts are head, eyes, ears, nose, mouth, hands, and legs.

Eyes help us to see things. Ears help us to hear sounds. The nose helps us smell. The tongue helps us taste food.

We should keep our body clean. We must wash our hands before eating food. We should brush our teeth every day and take a bath regularly.

Chapter: Plants Around Us

Plants are living things. They grow and need water and sunlight.

Plants have different parts such as roots, stem, leaves, flowers, and fruits.

Roots grow under the soil and hold the plant firmly. Leaves help the plant make food.

Plants give us fruits, vegetables, and oxygen.

Chapter: Animals Around Us

Animals are living things. They move, eat food, and grow.

Some animals live on land, like dogs, cats, and cows. Some animals live in water, like fish.

Animals help us in many ways. Cows give milk and dogs guard our houses.

We should be kind to animals.
